FAQs

Why should one consider taking Glucomap?

Glucomap is an Ayurvedic formulation that naturally manages blood sugar levels and alleviates symptoms associated with diabetes. It addresses conditions such as prediabetes, type 2 diabetes, and elevated blood sugar levels.

Is Glucomap a clinically proven solution for blood sugar management?

Glucomap has undergone clinical testing at AIIMS New Delhi and KGMU Lucknow in four different trials, which have established a reduction in HbA1c and fasting blood sugar levels.

How does Glucomap work to naturally manage blood sugar levels?

Glucomap is formulated with a synergistic combination of 25 herbs that work at the fundamental level to naturally restore pancreatic function and insulin production.

What are the key ingredients in Glucomap, and how do they help manage diabetes?

The key ingredients of Glucomap include: • Jamun: Lowers blood sugar levels. • Karela: Revives pancreatic health. • Neem: Decreases insulin resistance to improve glucose tolerance. • Bael: Regulates insulin production and enhances insulin sensitivity. • Arjuna: Reduces cholesterol levels.

How quickly can Glucomap reduce HbA1C and sugar levels?

Glucomap has been clinically proven to reduce HbA1c levels as well as fasting and post-meal blood glucose levels within three months. It also efficiently lowers triglycerides and cholesterol levels within six months and helps slow down the progression of diabetes and diabetic complications. For best results, it is recommended to consume it regularly for three months, alongside a proper diet and lifestyle.

HIs Glucomap beneficial for other diabetes complications?

Glucomap helps in improving triglyceride levels and cholesterol and alleviates symptoms like frequent urination and fatigue.

How does Glucomap slow down the progression of diabetes and diabetic complications?

In addition to Glucomap, you can consider consuming Triphala to flush out toxins from the body. Furthermore, consuming Neem can increase insulin sensitivity.

Can I take it with my regular allopathic medicine?

You can take Glucomap alongside allopathic medicine. However, it is advisable to monitor your sugar levels. If they decrease significantly, consult with a doctor. Glucomap addresses the root cause, so its effectiveness may become noticeable more slowly than allopathic medicine. Nonetheless, the results tend to be longer-lasting and contribute to moving towards a cure.

What is the recommended dosage for Glucomap, and how long should it be taken for best results?

The recommended dosage is two tablets twice a day for at least three months. For personalized guidance, you can also consult an expert Vaidya by calling our toll-free number at 1800-1020-230.

Who can benefit from using Glucomap, and for which conditions is it suitable?

Glucomap can benefit individuals with prediabetes, fasting glucose and post-meal conditions, type 2 diabetes, high HbA1c levels, and elevated sugar levels due to various reasons.
